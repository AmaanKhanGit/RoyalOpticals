FAQs

Q: Do you offer free frame adjustments or repairs?

A: Yes, we provide free frame adjustments for glasses purchased from Royal Opticals for the life of the product. Minor repairs may also be done free of charge.

Q: How long does it take for glasses to be ready?

A: Standard prescriptions usually take 5-7 working days. Complex or specialized lenses may take longer. We will notify you when they are ready.

Q: Can I get my eye prescription checked here?

A: Yes, we have certified optometrists available. Please use the 'Book Appointment' button above to schedule your eye exam.

Q: Can I use my old frames with new lenses?

A: Yes, in most cases, we can fit new lenses into your existing frames, provided the frames are in good structural condition. Our technician will assess them first.

Q: What should I bring to my eye exam appointment?

A: Please bring your current glasses/contact lenses, a list of any medications you are taking, and your vision insurance card (if applicable).
